# Last Call Insights: Tucson Nightlife Sentiment Analysis

*Note: This project was developed collaboratively by the NL Consulting team. This repository highlights my specific contributions to the overall Natural Language Processing application.*

## 📌 Project Overview
This project is an NLP-driven sentiment analysis of 1,226 Yelp reviews across 39 Tucson nightlife venues. The primary goal is to move beyond aggregate star ratings to identify true customer churn drivers and convert those insights into owner-ready business strategies. We sought to answer whether execution volatility and sentiment dispersion are stronger predictors of customer churn than average star ratings.

## 🛠️ My Contributions
As the **Business Insights Lead for Recommendations & Strategy**, my focus was translating raw data outputs into actionable business intelligence:
* **Strategic Framework Design:** Formulated the core business questions, assessing the gap between public star ratings and actual sentiment stability.
***Insight Generation:** Identified that while core product quality (food & drink) is strong across venues, execution volatility (service & atmosphere) is the primary driver of negative reviews.
**Performance Benchmarking:** Developed venue-specific profiles to illustrate how reducing execution dispersion protects rating strength and improves repeat customer stability.

## 📊 Key Findings
**Stars Mask Reality:** Star ratings overstate true sentiment across 22 of the 39 venues analyzed.
**Execution > Product:** Food and drink dominate 80.8% of reviews, meaning core product quality is generally not the problem. Service breaks the experience; it is low-volume but high-impact.
***Churn is Concentrated:** Dissatisfaction is highly concentrated, with a small number of venues generating the majority of negative signals (e.g., Prep & Pastry's Unhappiness Index was 1.6x higher than the next venue).
***Stability Wins Loyalty:** The market rewards reliable quality over average quality. Venues demonstrating premium consistency earn long-term loyalty gains.

## 💻 Methodology & Tech Stack
* **Data:** Yelp Academic Dataset (Tucson Nightlife).
***Sentiment Scoring:** VADER sentiment analysis.
**Topic Modeling:** Latent Dirichlet Allocation (LDA) resulting in 21 discovered conversation clusters.
  **Metrics:** Aspect-Based Sentiment Analysis (ABSA), Unhappiness Index, and Rating vs. Sentiment Gap analysis.
